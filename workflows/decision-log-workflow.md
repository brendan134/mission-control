# Decision Log Workflow

## Purpose
Create a repeatable way to capture important decisions so they do not get lost in chat.

## Goal
Record decisions that affect business direction, system behaviour, workflows, priorities, or model usage.

## When to Log a Decision
Log a decision when it changes:
- strategy
- priorities
- system rules
- model policy
- workflows
- project direction
- operating constraints
- important business choices

## Step 1: Identify the Decision
Define clearly:
- what was decided
- what is changing
- who it affects

## Step 2: Capture the Why
Record:
- why the decision was made
- what problem it solves
- what trade-off was accepted

## Step 3: Record the Impact
Capture:
- what files or systems are affected
- what behaviour changes
- what should happen next

## Step 4: Add Next Actions
Define:
- implementation steps
- file updates needed
- review point

## Step 5: Save It
Record the decision in:
- `systems/recent-decisions.md`

## Standard Decision Format
- Date:
- Decision:
- Why:
- Impact:
- Affected files / systems:
- Next actions:
- Review date:

## Operating Rule
If a decision affects how the business or system operates, log it.
Do not rely on memory or chat history.
