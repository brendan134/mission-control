# Content Production Workflow

## Purpose
Turn ideas, client insights, and business lessons into repeatable content assets.

## Goal
Create a simple system for producing useful content consistently.

## Step 1: Capture Ideas
Capture from:
- client conversations
- coaching insights
- recurring problems
- mistakes and lessons
- business observations
- team and leadership patterns

## Step 2: Select Angle
Choose one angle:
- problem
- mistake
- lesson
- framework
- story
- belief
- tactical advice

## Step 3: Define Audience and Outcome
- Who is this for?
- What problem does it address?
- What should they understand, feel, or do next?

## Step 4: Draft Core Message
Structure:
- hook
- problem
- insight
- practical shift
- next step or takeaway

## Step 5: Choose Format
Possible formats:
- social post
- email
- article
- workshop outline
- client note
- lead magnet seed

## Step 6: Review
Check for:
- clarity
- relevance
- practical value
- strong positioning
- alignment with Leader By Design

## Step 7: Repurpose
Convert strong content into:
- multiple posts
- email content
- workshop ideas
- frameworks
- sales assets
- client resources

## Operating Rules
- capture raw ideas quickly
- refine later
- prioritise usefulness over cleverness
- create once, reuse multiple times
- save strong outputs as reusable assets
