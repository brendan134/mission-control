# High-Impact Leader Podcast SEO Execution Plan

## Purpose
Increase podcast discoverability and downloads through structured, ongoing SEO improvement.

## Desired Outcome
- move from approximately 15–20 weekly downloads to 500–1000 weekly downloads
- create a repeatable podcast SEO improvement process

## Execution Focus
- audit the current baseline
- identify the highest-leverage SEO improvements
- implement improvements consistently
- track results and iterate

## Workstreams

### 1. Baseline Audit
Outcome:
- current podcast SEO baseline understood clearly

Actions:
- review show title, episode titles, descriptions, keywords, categories, and distribution setup
- identify obvious gaps and quick wins
- document the current baseline metrics

### 2. On-Page Optimisation
Outcome:
- stronger search and discovery signals for episodes and show-level assets

Actions:
- improve episode naming
- improve descriptions and keyword relevance
- improve metadata consistency

### 3. Distribution and Visibility
Outcome:
- stronger reach across platforms and discovery surfaces

Actions:
- review podcast platform setup
- improve visibility across directories and supporting assets
- identify ways to support discoverability outside the core feed

### 4. Review and Iteration
Outcome:
- optimisation becomes a repeatable process rather than a one-off push

Actions:
- review download patterns weekly
- track what changes correlate with better results
- continue improving titles, descriptions, and supporting content

## Immediate Next Actions
- [ ] audit current show and episode SEO setup
- [ ] identify the highest-leverage metadata changes
- [ ] define a weekly SEO improvement cadence
- [ ] establish simple baseline metrics for review

## Metrics
- weekly downloads
- show discoverability signals
- episode-level performance trends
- consistency of optimisation work

## Risks
- growth may take longer than expected to compound
- SEO changes may need sustained effort before impact is visible
- distribution limitations may cap growth unless addressed

## Review Rhythm
- weekly optimisation review
- monthly growth review
- quarter-end review against target download range
