# Leader By Design 1:1 Service Upgrade Execution Plan

## Purpose
Strengthen the 1:1 coaching offer so clients clearly experience premium, white-glove service and strong value.

## Desired Outcome
- a clearly defined premium service standard
- consistent white-glove experience across the full client journey
- stronger client confidence in value received

## Execution Focus
- define the full client journey
- identify service gaps
- improve service touchpoints
- align delivery, communication, and perceived value

## Workstreams

### 1. Client Journey Mapping
Outcome:
- full current journey documented from onboarding to delivery and review

Actions:
- map every client touchpoint
- identify gaps, inconsistencies, and friction points
- clarify what the ideal experience should feel like

### 2. White-Glove Service Standard
Outcome:
- premium service standard clearly defined

Actions:
- define service principles
- define minimum service touchpoints
- define what makes the experience feel high-value and premium

### 3. Delivery Improvement
Outcome:
- stronger client-facing experience in real delivery

Actions:
- improve communication rhythm
- improve preparation, follow-up, and accountability touchpoints
- tighten delivery consistency

### 4. Offer and Value Clarity
Outcome:
- clients clearly understand what they are receiving and why it is valuable

Actions:
- review how the service is described
- align promise with actual delivery
- identify ways to make value more visible

## Immediate Next Actions
- [ ] map the current client journey end to end
- [ ] define white-glove service standards
- [ ] identify the biggest service gaps
- [ ] list 3-5 changes that would most improve perceived value

## Metrics
- client experience consistency
- clarity of service delivery
- confidence in value delivered
- retention / satisfaction signals

## Risks
- service improvements staying conceptual instead of operational
- delivery upgrades not being documented clearly enough to repeat

## Review Rhythm
- weekly improvement review
- monthly service quality review
- quarter-end review against client experience goals
