**What it is:** Structure for strategic planning sessions
**Content:**
- Opening: Review last quarter results (what worked/didn't)

**What it is:** Framework for moving team members between roles
**Content:**
- Current role and skills
- Target role and skills needed
- Transition timeline (weeks/months)
- Training and development activities
- Check-in points (weekly one-on-ones)
- Success criteria at each stage
- Final evaluation date
