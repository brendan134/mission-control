### LinkedIn Post

--- POST 1 ---
The biggest mistake leaders make? Letting their team say "I'll do it next week" without consequence.

Here's what works:
- What: One priority for the week
- How: Exact steps to achieve it
- When: Time blocked in calendar

If they can't explain HOW they'll do it, they won't do it. #LeaderByDesign #Leadership

--- POST 2 ---
Just helped a leader break down a massive strategic project into smaller "green belt" projects.

The secret? Don't try to eat the whole elephant at once.

Pick one section, go deep, implement, then move to the next.

What's your biggest project right now that's stalling? #LeaderByDesign #BusinessGrowth

--- POST 3 ---
Managing someone who's been there longer than you? It's one of the hardest leadership challenges.

Here's what works:
- Be direct but compassionate
- Use questions, not commands
- Set clear expectations consistently
- Follow up EVERY time (if you let it slide once, it continues)

What leadership challenge are you facing? #LeaderByDesign #TeamLeadership
