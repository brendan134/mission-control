### Market Analysis (with Richard)

### Elimination Process - Where NOT to Play
- **Playgrounds**: Contracting work, different business model, tight cash flow
- **Back to School**: Highly seasonal, no brand equity
- **Textbooks**: Seasonal, school space only
- **ICT/Digital AV**: Predominantly schools
- **Health & Hygiene**: Low margin (debating)

### Potential Where to Play
- **Arts & Crafts**: $77M market, strategic (customers buy every 2 weeks = lock in)
- **Sensory**: $250-290M potential if government funds sensory rooms in mainstream schools
- **Furniture**: Different tables/chairs for different ages
- **Sport & Outdoor**: Some overlap
- **Educational Resources**: Some overlap
- **ELC Projects**: $70M market

### Target Market Calculation
- If focus on ELC + OOSH: $645M market → need 25% market share (tough)
- If include schools: $1B market → need 10% market share (easier)
- Alby's preference: Target $1B pool, aim for 10%

- **How to Win**: Point of difference, capabilities needed
