### 2. Delegation & Leadership Stages
- **Stage 1 - Directing:** Specific instructions, tell them what to do
- **Stage 2 - Coaching:** Ask questions, side by side, holding their hand
- **Stage 3 - Supporting:** Reverse coaching, they lead you
- **Stage 4 - Delegating:** Confident, handle 80-90% independently
- Chad needs to assess where his team is and adjust accordingly
