### Done-For-You Recommendations

### 1. New Hire 30-60-90 Day Plan Template
**What it is:** Structured onboarding plan for Tom and Matt
**Content:**
**Days 1-30: Learn the Business**
- Week 1: Meet everyone, understand company structure, shadow key people
- Week 2: Learn products/services, visit key sites, understand customer base
- Week 3: Start owning 1-2 small areas, get feedback daily
- Week 4: First 30-day review with manager

**Days 31-60: Build Competence**
- Own specific areas independently
- Weekly one-on-ones
- Start contributing to team meetings
- Identify quick wins

**Days 61-90: Deliver Results**
- Full ownership of role
- Contribute to strategy discussions
- 90-day review + performance agreement finalised

### 2. Month-End Planning Meeting Agenda
**What it is:** Agenda for finance team month-end planning
**Content:**
**Week Before Month End:**
- Review outstanding invoices
- Flag any delays or issues
- Confirm team availability
- Assign specific tasks

**Agenda:**
1. Review outstanding items (10 min)
2. Assign responsibilities (10 min)
3. Timeline and deadlines (5 min)
4. Escalation path (5 min)
5. Open discussion (10 min)

### 3. Termination Risk Checklist
**What it is:** Checklist to protect business when terminating employees
**Content:**
**During Probation:**
- [ ] Document performance issues in writing
- [ ] Give clear feedback and opportunity to improve
- [ ] Keep signed copies of all performance documentation
- [ ] Have HR review before termination decision

**At Termination:**
- [ ] Prepare deed of release
- [ ] Offer something (e.g., reference) in exchange for release
- [ ] Document everything
- [ ] Have witness present
- [ ] Consider payout to avoid dispute
