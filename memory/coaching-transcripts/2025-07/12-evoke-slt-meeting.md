### 2. New Process Implementation - Change Register
**Success Story - Cedar Mountain:**
- Lando presented change register to client Daniel
- Budget went up $117K but client was prepared
- Client could see exactly what added scope cost
- Could make informed decisions about what to keep/remove
- **Key insight:** Managing expectations upfront vs. surprises at end

**Change Register Framework:**
- Two columns: "Approved/Requested" vs "Additional Scope"
- Updated throughout design process, not just at end
- Shows clients exactly what's changed and cost impact
- Became "potential sales tool" - demonstrates professionalism
- Pre-con to add costs, designers to document changes
