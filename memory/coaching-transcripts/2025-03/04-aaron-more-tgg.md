### Done-For-You Recommendations

### 1. Weekly Team Check-in Questions Template
**What it is:** Structured questions for weekly one-on-ones
**Content:**
- How have you gone in the last 7 days?
- How have you been going with your performance agreement?
- Any blocks or challenges?
- What's top priority for next week?
- Any support you need from me?

### 2. Performance Agreement Template
**What it is:** One-page performance agreement framework
**Content:**
- Purpose: How role contributes to organisation
- Autonomy: What decisions can you make?
- Mastery: Key metrics (3-5 max)
- Examples: Case notes, outreach calls, training attendance

### 3. Conflict Resolution Script - Joe Situation
**What it is:** Framework for addressing difficult colleague behavior
**Content:**
- Separate 1:1s to understand perspectives
- Focus on behaviors, not personality
- Ground rules: No interrupting, solutions not blame
- Key questions: What's the impact? What's needed?
- Document conversation for HR if needed
