## VALUE ADD SECTION

### Done-For-You Recommendations

### 1. Board Presentation Preparation Checklist
**What it is:** Pre-presentation checklist
**Content:**
- Review all slides for clarity
- Practice verbal delivery
- Prepare for Q&A
- Have backup slides ready
- Confirm attendees and format

### 2. "What's Got to Give" Framework
**What it is:** Template for pushing back on additional work
**Content:**
- List current commitments
- Identify impact of adding new work
- Prepare alternative options
- Frame as: "I can do X, but Y will suffer. What's priority?"
- Document conversation

### 3. Recruitment Risk Assessment
**What it is:** Checklist for evaluating new hires
**Content:**
- Verify track record (call previous employers)
- Check visa/work rights
- Assess cultural fit
- Understand reasons for leaving
- Consider trial period or project-based start
