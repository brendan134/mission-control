## VALUE ADD SECTION

### Done-For-You Recommendations

### 1. Leadership Team Conflict Resolution Script
**What it is:** Framework for mediating two senior leaders not speaking
**Content:**
- Separate 1:1s with each person to understand perspectives
- Bring both together in facilitated meeting
- Ground rules: No interrupting, focus on solutions
- Key questions: What's the impact on the team? What's needed to move forward?
- Outcome: Clear commitment or escalation path

### 2. EOS Quarterly Review Preparation Checklist
**What it is:** Pre-quarterly review checklist
**Content:**
- Review all rocks from current quarter
- Mark complete/carry over/incomplete
- Prepare new rocks with clear owners and deadlines
- Ensure each rock is measurable
- Confirm attendance and agenda

### 3. Daily Summary Template
**What it is:** Structured end-of-day reflection
**Content:**
- What did I accomplish today?
- What did I learn?
- What am I grateful for?
- What's for tomorrow (top 3 priorities)?
- Any blocks or needs?
