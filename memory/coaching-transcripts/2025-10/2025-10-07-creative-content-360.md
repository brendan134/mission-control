## Call Summary

This was a discovery/exploration call between Brendan and Rebecca Hinojosa from Creative Content 360 (CC360), a course creator platform. Rebecca was exploring whether to migrate from Kajabi to CC360.

**Key topics discussed:**
- Rebecca's health update (iron infusion, upcoming surgery)
- CC360 platform features and pricing (~$1,428/year vs Kajabi's ~$500/month)
- Migration process and benefits
- Comparison with other tools (HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, Zapier)
- Domain management and unlimited domains
- Automation and workflow capabilities
- Calendar integration
- Website building features
- Quiz and lead magnet functionality
- Support and customer service

**Notable insights:**
- CC360 is described as "Kajabi times 10" with more functionality
- Unlimited contacts and domains
- All-in-one platform replaces multiple subscriptions (Kajabi, ActiveCampaign, Calendly, etc.)
- 24/7 chat support with real people
- Domain migration costs ~US$35 per domain
- AI-assisted content creation for sales pages and funnels

**Outcome:** Rebecca was interested in potentially switching after the Vet Summit, to avoid disrupting operations during busy periods.
