### The Hook

Here's what I see all the time with business owners:

You started your business because you were brilliant at something. Really good at what you do.

And that business grew. Maybe faster than you expected.

Revenue came in. You hired team members. Suddenly you're not just doing the work — you're responsible for people.

But here's the thing about growth: it doesn't wait until you're ready.

Now you're making decisions on the fly. Your best people are waiting for clarity you're too busy to give. Your calendar is chaos.

You built this business for freedom.

Instead, you've never been more trapped.

This episode is for leaders ready to stop carrying everything themselves and start designing a leadership system that scales.
