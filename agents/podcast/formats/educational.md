# Episode Format: Educational (What To Do)

**Format:** Educational (What To Do)
**Purpose:** Teach a leadership behaviour or decision process through structured explanation

---

## Structure (9 Steps)

### 1. HOOK (20-30 sec)
- Present the problem or challenge
- Create urgency/curiosity
- "Here's what most leaders miss..."

### 2. THE PROBLEM (1-2 min)
- Define the challenge clearly
- Show who experiences this
- Why it matters now

### 3. WHY IT'S HARD (1-2 min)
- The obstacle
- What makes it difficult
- Why simple advice doesn't work

### 4. THE FRAMEWORK (2-3 min)
- Present the main model/framework
- Number the steps/stages
- Give each element a clear name

### 5. DEEP DIVE: STEP 1 (1-2 min)
- Explain first element
- What it looks like
- Common mistakes

### 6. DEEP DIVE: STEPS 2-3 (2-3 min)
- Continue through framework
- Balance explanation with examples
- Build momentum

### 7. PUTTING IT TOGETHER (1-2 min)
- How the pieces connect
- The sequence matters
- What success looks like

### 8. REFLECTION
- "Where are you in this process?"
- "What's the gap?"
- Self-assessment prompt

### 9. CLOSE
- Key takeaway
- Soft CTA (next steps, resources)
- Signature sign-off

---

## Example: "The 4 Stages of Delegation"

**Framework:**
1. Clarify (what, why, how well)
2. Assign (who, when, check-in)
3. Support (resources, remove blocks)
4. Review (measure, feedback, escalate)

---

## Pre-Write Questions (Must Answer)

1. What's the one thing I'm teaching?
2. Why is this hard for leaders?
3. What's the framework/structure?
4. What are the key steps/stages?
5. What does success look like?
6. What primary keyword anchors this?
7. What secondary keywords support it?
8. Primary objection: "This won't work for me" - address
9. Secondary objection: "I don't have time" - address

---

## Validation Checklist

- [ ] Problem clearly defined
- [ ] Framework present and named
- [ ] Each step explained
- [ ] Common mistakes noted
- [ ] Success criteria given
- [ ] Both objections addressed
- [ ] Primary keyword used
- [ ] Length: 10-13 min
- [ ] Tone: quiet authority

---

## Episode 15 Assessment (Educational)

- ✅ Framework: Clarify → Delegate → Systematize → Elevate
- ✅ Steps explained clearly
- ⚠️ Needs stronger problem statement in hook
- ⚠️ Reflection section could be sharper
