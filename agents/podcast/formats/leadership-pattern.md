# Episode Format: Leadership Pattern

**Format:** Leadership Pattern
**Purpose:** Identify recurring leadership breakdowns and reframe them through structural insight

---

## Structure (9 Steps - LOCKED)

### 1. Opening Hook (20-30 seconds)
- Name the felt experience of the listener
- No hype, no stats, no personal backstory
- Make the listener think: "This is exactly my situation"

---

### 2. The Leadership Pattern
- Clearly describe the recurring leadership pattern
- Use language leaders would actually say
- Normalise the behaviour (no blame, no judgement)

---

### 3. How This Shows Up Day-to-Day (REQUIRED)
- Describe subtle, everyday signs the pattern is present
- Use inner-dialogue language leaders recognise
- This section should create recognition, not instruction

---

### 4. Why This Pattern Persists
- Explain why the pattern makes sense at earlier stages
- Show why it breaks down as teams and complexity grow
- Focus on systems and leadership design, not effort or motivation

---

### 5. What This Costs Over Time (REQUIRED)
- Calmly articulate the longer-term consequences
- Focus on leadership load, decision drag, team confidence, energy
- No fear, no drama — just clear cause and effect

---

### 6. What High-Impact Leaders Do Differently
- Shift from "fixing" to designing leadership
- Emphasise clarity, ownership, decision rights, rhythm
- Avoid step-by-step tactics, models, or frameworks

---

### 7. Reflection Moment
- Ask 1-2 thoughtful questions
- Encourage awareness and insight
- No action plans, no "do this next"

---

### 8. Explicit Episode CTA (Contextual)
- Clearly mention the High-Impact Leader Club
- Position it as the place to go deeper, not a sales pitch
- Calm, confident, matter-of-fact

---

### 9. Close with Signature Line (LOCKED - EXACT)
Design your leadership.
Build your team.
Lead with impact.

---

## Language & Style Rules (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

| Must Do | Must NOT Do |
|---------|-------------|
| Calm, confident, reflective | Hype or marketing language |
| Short paragraphs | Buzzwords or clichés |
| Clear, human language | Excessive personal stories |
| Speak to the leader, not at them | "10 tips" or numbered lists |

**Tone:** Pattern-based, practical, grounded, senior
**Audience:** Experienced business owners and leaders (not beginners)

---

## Positioning (LOCKED)

This is NOT a:
- Hustle podcast
- Motivation podcast
- Tactics podcast

This IS about:
- Designing leadership, not fixing business problems
- High-impact leaders don't push harder — they redesign clarity, ownership, and rhythm

---

## Pre-Write Questions (Must Answer)

1. What is the recurring leadership pattern?
2. What does it feel like for the listener?
3. How does it show up day-to-day?
4. Why does it persist?
5. What does it cost over time?
6. What do high-impact leaders do differently?
7. What reflection question(s) will I ask?
8. Primary keyword for this episode?
9. Secondary keywords?
