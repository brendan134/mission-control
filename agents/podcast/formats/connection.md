# Episode Format: Connection

**Format:** Connection (Emotional)
**Purpose:** Build trust with business owners and leaders through emotional resonance - make the listener feel deeply understood
**Core Rule:** Do NOT solve the problem. Do NOT teach frameworks. Do NOT explain how.
**Goal:** The listener thinks: "That's exactly what I'm dealing with."

---

## Structure (10 Steps - LOCKED)

### 1. Opening Hook (0:00-0:45)
- Goal: Immediate relevance and pattern interrupt
- Call out a specific moment or experience
- Make it feel personal and real
- Slow down delivery slightly to land the moment

### 2. Standard Podcast Intro (0:45-1:30)
- Welcome listeners to the High-Impact Leader Podcast
- Brief value proposition
- Set up the episode topic

### 3. Context Setup (1:30-3:00)
- Goal: Clearly define the situation
- Describe when and where this shows up
- Use specific, observable behaviours
- Include frequency or repetition
- **Focus:** Help the listener see their own world

### 4. Agitation / Layered Pain (3:00-5:30)
- Goal: Deepen emotional connection
- Expand the frustration
- Show internal conflict
- Highlight hidden costs
- **Layer the impact across:** Leader, Team, Business
- **Important:** This section is longer - sit in the pain, do not rush

### 5. Relatable Story or Pattern (5:30-7:30)
- Goal: Build trust through lived experience
- **Options:** Personal story, client example, repeated pattern you see
- **Structure:** Situation → Struggle → Realisation (no solution)
- **Instruction:** Let the story breathe. This is where connection locks in.

### 6. Identity Mirror (7:30-9:00)
- Goal: Reflect who they are or have become
- Name the pattern directly
- Highlight the gap between intention and reality
- Speak clearly and honestly
- **Examples:**
  - "You've become the bottleneck… without meaning to"
  - "You're carrying more than you should"

### 7. Truth Statement (9:00-10:00)
- Goal: Light reframe without teaching
- Keep it simple
- Shift perspective slightly
- No frameworks or breakdowns
- **Examples:**
  - "It's not a capability problem"
  - "It's not about working harder"

### 8. What High-Impact Leaders Do (10:00-10:40)
- Goal: Identity elevation (not instruction)
- Max 3 sentences
- Behaviour-based only
- No steps, no frameworks
- Must contrast the struggle
- **Template:**
  - "High-Impact Leaders don't [common behaviour].
    They [different behaviour].
    They [identity-based way of operating]."

### 9. Future Possibility (10:40-12:00)
- Goal: Show what better looks like
- Focus on feeling and state
- Describe a different way of operating
- Keep it simple and visual
- **Examples:**
  - "Where your team moves without you"
  - "Where decisions don't sit with you"

### 10. Reflection Question (12:00-13:00)
- Goal: Create self-awareness
- Ask ONE clear question
- Give space after asking (in delivery)
- **Examples:**
  - "Where is this showing up for you?"
  - "What are you still holding onto?"

## Delivery Rules (MANDATORY)

### Language
- Simple and direct
- Short sentences
- No jargon
- No em dashes
- Conversational tone

### DO NOT INCLUDE
- Step-by-step solutions
- Framework teaching
- Over-explaining
- Multiple competing ideas

### MUST INCLUDE
- Specific real scenarios
- Observable behaviours
- Emotional depth
- Identity-level reflection

---

## "What High-Impact Leaders Do" Rules
- Max 3 sentences
- Behaviour only
- No "how"
- No frameworks
- Must contrast the listener's current behaviour
- Must feel aspirational, not instructional

---

## Pacing Principles (For 10-13 Minutes)
- Slow down the agitation and story sections
- Do NOT fill time with teaching
- Use pauses to let ideas land
- Depth over volume
- One core idea per episode

---

## Quality Control Check

### Connection Check
1. Does this make the listener feel understood within the first 2 minutes?
2. Are the scenarios specific and real?

### Depth Check
3. Did we fully explore the problem (not rush it)?
4. Does the story feel real and relatable?

### Discipline Check
5. Did we avoid solving or teaching?
6. Did we avoid frameworks and steps?

### Identity Check
7. Does the Identity Mirror clearly reflect the listener?
8. Does "What High-Impact Leaders Do" elevate without teaching?

### Structure Check
9. Is there exactly ONE reflection question?
10. Does the CTA feel natural and aligned?

---

## One-Line Operating Principle

> "Go deeper into their experience, not wider into your knowledge."
