# Episode Format: Connection

**Format:** Connection
**Purpose:** Connect two leadership ideas and reveal the relationship between them

---

## Structure (9 Steps)

### 1. HOOK (20-30 sec)
- Present an intriguing question or tension
- "What if X and Y were connected?"

### 2. IDEA ONE (2 min)
- Introduce first concept
- Why it matters
- How leaders typically think about it

### 3. THE DISCONNECT (1 min)
- What's missing from thinking about idea one alone?
- Where do leaders go wrong?

### 4. IDEA TWO (2 min)
- Introduce second concept
- Why it matters
- How leaders typically think about it

### 5. THE CONNECTION (2-3 min)
- How these ideas relate
- The missing link
- Why together they create something new

### 6. PRACTICAL IMPLICATION (2-3 min)
- What leaders should do differently
- How to apply both together
- The compound effect

### 7. PROOF / EXAMPLE (1-2 min)
- Real example showing both in action
- Before/after
- Specific outcome

### 8. REFLECTION
- "Which of these do you already have?"
- "What's missing?"
- The invitation to integrate

### 9. CLOSE
- Crystallise the connection
- Soft CTA
- Signature sign-off

---

## Example Connection: "Clarity + Accountability"

**Idea One:** Clarity (clear vision, expectations, direction)
**Idea Two:** Accountability (ownership, follow-through, consequences)
**Connection:** Clarity without accountability creates frustration. Accountability without clarity creates resentment. Together they create high-performing teams.

---

## Pre-Write Questions (Must Answer)

1. What's the first idea?
2. What's the second idea?
3. How are they currently thought about separately?
4. What's the hidden connection?
5. What does ignoring the connection cost?
6. How do they amplify each other?
7. What primary keyword anchors this?
8. What secondary keywords support it?
9. What objections might arise?

---

## Validation Checklist

- [ ] Both ideas clearly defined
- [ ] Connection explicitly stated
- [ ] Practical application given
- [ ] Proof example included
- [ ] Both objections addressed
- [ ] Primary keyword used
- [ ] Length: 10-13 min
- [ ] Tone: quiet authority
