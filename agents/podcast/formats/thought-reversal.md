# Episode Format: Thought Reversal

**Format:** Thought Reversal
**Purpose:** Flip a common leadership belief and replace it with stronger design thinking
**Subtypes:** OG, Context Switch, Self-Identify

---

## Structure (9 Steps)

### 1. HOOK (20-30 sec)
- Present the common belief
- Create tension by showing the problem it creates

### 2. THE COMMON BELIEF (1-2 min)
- State what most people believe
- Acknowledge why it seems true
- Show who's promoting this idea

### 3. THE REALITY CHECK (2 min)
- What actually happens when you follow this belief?
- Cost #1: What you lose
- Cost #2: What your team loses

### 4. WHY IT PERSISTS (1-2 min)
- Cultural reinforcement
- It's easier than the alternative
- No one teaches the replacement

### 5. THE THOUGHT REVERSAL (2 min)
- Flip the belief
- Introduce the structural alternative
- Name the new principle

### 6. STRUCTURAL INSIGHT (2-3 min)
- How this actually works
- The design behind the shift
- What replaces the old belief

### 7. PROOF POINTS (1-2 min)
- Example from coaching/pattern observed
- Before/after structure
- Specific outcome

### 8. REFLECTION
- Prompt for self-assessment
- "What if you've been thinking about this wrong?"
- The invitation to shift

### 9. CLOSE
- Crystallise the shift
- Soft CTA (listen to more, reach out)
- Signature sign-off

---

## Example: "Working Harder = Success" Reversal

**Common Belief:** "Work harder, get ahead"
**Reversal:** "Design smarter, scale further"

---

## Pre-Write Questions (Must Answer)

1. What is the common belief I'm flipping?
2. Why does it feel true?
3. What does following this belief cost?
4. What's the structural alternative?
5. How do I prove this works?
6. What primary keyword anchors this?
7. What secondary keywords support it?
8. Primary objection: "But X" - how do I address?
9. Secondary objection: "I don't have time" - how do I address?

---

## Validation Checklist

- [ ] Common belief clearly stated
- [ ] Cost of following belief quantified
- [ ] Reversal clearly articulated
- [ ] Structural insight provided
- [ ] Proof point included
- [ ] Both objections addressed
- [ ] Primary keyword used
- [ ] Length: 10-13 min
- [ ] Tone: quiet authority
