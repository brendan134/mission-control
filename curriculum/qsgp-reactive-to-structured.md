# Quarterly Strategic Growth Plan (QSGP)
## Reactive → Structured Leadership
### 12 Staff | Owner Working Late, Chasing Updates, Losing Team
---
## 1. Last Quarter Review
**Goal:** Move from reactive to structured leadership. **Achieved?** No.
**What got in way:** Owner firefighting daily, no rhythm, no decision authority given.
**What must change:** Owner must protect strategic time; decisions must be explicit.
---
## 2. This Quarter's ONE Goal
**Strategic goal:** Reduce owner firefighting to create space for strategic leadership.
**Measurable outcome:** Owner works max 1 urgent item per day by Day 90.
**Success:** Team solves problems without owner; owner leaves by 6pm.
---
## 3. Focus Areas
**PRIMARY (Next 30 Days):** Leadership Rhythm
- Mon/Wed/Fri structure creates predictability
- Reduces daily interruptions by 50%
- Makes team self-organize
**SECONDARY (Days 31-60):** Decision Rights
**LATER (Days 61-90):** Development Conversations
---
## 4. Next 30 Days — PRIMARY FOCUS: Leadership Rhythm
*(All 3 actions directly support this focus)*
1. **Monday Priorities** — Team sets weekly priorities without owner (30 min). Owner does not attend.
2. **Wednesday Blockers** — 15-min check for stuck items only. No status updates.
3. **Friday Review** — 30-min: what worked, what didn't, who needs help next week.
---
## 5. Leader Support
- Accountability: Coach checks if Mon/Wed/Fri happened each week
- Decision support: Coach available only for Level 3-4 decisions
- Focus: Help owner resist "jumping in" when team struggles
---
*Designed for reactive leader under time pressure. Simple. Repeatable. Execute.*
