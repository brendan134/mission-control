# Content Batch

Weekly content creation for LinkedIn and newsletter.

## What This Does

Creates 3 LinkedIn posts + 1 newsletter segment based on Leader By Design IP.

## Input Required

1. **Which Core IP to anchor to:**
   - Leader By Design
   - Leadership Load
   - Self-Managing Teams
   - Leadership Rhythm
   - High-Impact Growth Path

2. **Current business context:**
   - Any wins/case studies?
   - Common client challenges?
   - Upcoming events?

3. **Hook style preference:**
   - Question-based
   - Statement/controversial
   - Story-led
   - Data-driven

## Output

Saves to: `/workspace/content/weekly-content-YYYY-MM-DD.md`

Format:
```markdown
# Weekly Content Batch - 2026-04-17

## Post 1: [Topic]
Hook: [Scroll-stopping opener]
Body: [150-200 words]
CTA: [Optional]

## Post 2: ...

## Post 3: ...

## Newsletter Segment
[50-100 words]
```

## Quick Template

### LinkedIn Post Structure
- **Hook** (first line - most important)
- **Body** (context, proof, value)
- **CTA** (engagement question)

### Hook Formulas
1. "I just [action] and [result]. Here's what I learned:"
2. "The biggest mistake leaders make with [topic] is..."
3. "Nobody talks about [topic], but here's why it matters:"
4. "[Number] days into [experiment], here's the update:"
5. "A client asked me [question] last week. My answer surprised them."

### Content Pillars (rotate through)
1. **Leadership** - team management, accountability
2. **Systems** - processes, automation, delegation
3. **Growth** - scaling, revenue, performance
4. ** mindset** - owner mentality, decision making

## Tips

- Keep posts under 300 words
- One idea per post
- End with a question to drive engagement
- Use line breaks - people scan
- Avoid jargon - write like you speak
