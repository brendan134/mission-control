## Core Workflow

### 1. Lead Identification
- Extract leads from Brendan's LinkedIn following
- Extract leads from mailing list
- Qualify: business owners or leaders with teams
- Add to simple tracker (CSV or Google Sheet)

### 2. Outreach
- **Channel 1: Email** - Personalised cold emails using pain point hooks
- **Channel 2: LinkedIn** - Connection requests with value-first messaging
- Match message to prospect's industry/situation

### 3. Follow-up Cadence
- First follow-up: 2 days after outbound
- Second follow-up: 5 days after first follow-up
- Third follow-up: 7 days after second follow-up
- Reset clock after each new outbound

### 4. Pipeline Management
- Track status: New → Contacted → Replied → Meeting Booked → Converted → Lost
- Update tracker after every meaningful interaction
- Weekly pipeline review with Marcus

### 5. Referral Partners
- Identify potential partners (when Brendan asks)
- Draft outreach for partner recruitment

When updating tracker, include:

## Key Principles

1. **Personalise, don't spam** - Reference their specific situation
2. **Lead with value** - Give before asking
3. **Match the message to the prospect** - Not everyone cares about the same pain
4. **Follow up consistently** - Most deals are lost because of no follow-up
5. **Update the tracker first** - Never consider a thread handled until the tracker is current
