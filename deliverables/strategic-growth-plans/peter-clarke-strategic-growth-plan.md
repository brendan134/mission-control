### 90-Day Strategic Action Plan

The next 90 days should focus on redesigning your role and the operational structure of Kwik Kopy.

The goal is not rapid expansion.

The goal is clarity, alignment and leverage.

---

#### Phase 1 — Leadership Role Redesign

**Weeks 1–4**

The first priority is removing the biggest energy drains from your role.

**Key Actions:**

1. **Define Nicola's expanded role** — Create a Centre Manager performance agreement that clarifies her ownership of:
   - Day-to-day operations
   - Staff management
   - Customer service delivery
   - First-line escalation

2. **Establish decision channels** — Implement a clear protocol: "Have you spoken to Nicola first?"
   - Train the team to default to Nicola, not you
   - Build Nicola's confidence to make operational decisions

3. **Begin stepping back from operational decisions** — Your time must start shifting toward BD and strategic activities

**Success Metric by Week 4:** Peter involved in < 3 operational decisions per day (down from current ~10+)

## Page 7 — 90-Day Plan Continued

#### Phase 2 — Client Opportunity System

**Weeks 5–8**

The second phase focuses on unlocking the hidden growth opportunities inside your existing client base.

**Key Actions:**

1. **Identify top 20 client opportunities** — Review your client list and identify accounts with highest growth potential

2. **Create a Client Review Framework** — For each high-potential client, ask:
   - What other services could we provide?
   - What's preventing them from spending more?
   - Who else in their organisation should we know?

3. **Implement proactive outreach** — Schedule quarterly strategic reviews with top 10 clients

4. **Upskill Nicola on relationship management** — Begin developing her capability to handle client conversations

**Success Metric by Week 8:** At least 5 strategic client reviews completed; 2-3 new opportunities identified

---

#### Phase 3 — Leadership Rhythm

**Weeks 9–12**

The final phase establishes a leadership rhythm that protects your time for strategic work.

**Calendar Structure:**

- Block at least **two days per week** for BD activities only
- No operational meetings during BD time
- These days should be used for:
  - Client meetings and relationship building
  - Strategic planning
  - Developing new service offers
  - Mentoring Nicola on leadership

This is where your Invention and Discernment geniuses become the biggest drivers of Kwik Kopy growth.

**Success Metric by Week 12:** Minimum 2 protected BD days per week; revenue from existing client growth up 10%
