# Coaching Call Value Add Generator

Create a personalized "massive value add" for the client after each coaching call.

---

## Input

- Client name:
- Industry/business type:
- Key insight from call:
- Specific challenge discussed:
- Framework(s) mentioned:
- Next step they committed to:

---

## Value Add Template

### Subject Line Options
- [ ] "Following up on today's call - [their name]"
- [ ] "Your [specific thing] - next steps"
- [ ] "[Insight from call] - here's your action plan"

---

## Structure

**1. Acknowledge & Reinforce**
- Reference their key insight or realization
- Validate their progress

**2. Their Personalized Recommendation**
- Based on what we discussed
- Specific to their situation (not generic)
- Could include:
  - Framework application
  - Template they can use
  - Resource that matches their challenge
  - Specific question to ask their team

**3. Clear Next Step**
- One action they committed to
- Deadline if mentioned

**4. Offer Support**
- How they can reach you
- Reminder of your availability

---

## Example

**Subject**: "Chad - your checklist idea + what we discussed"

Hey Chad,

That checklist approach you mentioned - giving staff specific areas to self-assess rather than a blank slate - is genuinely innovative. Most leaders ask "how do you feel about your role?" and get nothing useful. You've found a way to get real data while making it safe.

**Here's the template I'd use:**

[Insert checklist template adapted from call]

**Your next step:** Run the first one with whoever's there on Friday, even if it's only 2-3 team members. 85% attendance is still valuable data.

Let me know how it goes.

Cheers,
Brendan

---

## Important Notes

- Keep it to one page max
- Send within 24 hours of call
- Make it personal - reference their exact words
- Include ONE clear call to action
- No generic tips - everything tailored to their conversation
