- Handling objections in marketing messages
- Creating engaging social media content
- Writing landing page copy
- Crafting webinar or presentation scripts
- Any marketing or promotional writing

## How to Apply

1. **Identify the goal** - What do you want the reader to do?
2. **Select a card** - Match the situation to the card(s) above
3. **Follow the formula** - Use the structure provided
4. **Personalize** - Make it authentic to your brand voice
5. **Test** - Not every card works for every audience
