# Brendan Rogers — Extended Personal Story

## Extended Story Content

### Dad's Business Journey
- Entrepreneur, meat exporting business
- Seemed to be an empathetic leader — staff looked up to him, clients trusted him
- Business partner was a "scum bag and thief" — caused financial hardship, nearly bankrupted family
- Dad had to remortgage home and investment property to recover
- Had nervous breakdown and anxiety, fought back, got off medication
- Retired early as self-funded retiree for 25+ years
- Still volunteers at St Vincent De Paul each week
- Still runs Blackall school fete stall (raised $100K+ over 10 years)

### Key Lessons from Dad
1. **There's always a solution** — Not always clear at the time, but keep at it
2. **Indecision kills** — You're not always certain of the right way, but be committed
3. **People don't like indecisive leaders** — It erodes trust
4. **From Mum: Patience + persistence** — Do things enough times to build confidence

### Dad's Sacrifices
- Time with family (worked Saturdays in butcher shop too)
- Personal wellbeing (nervous breakdowns, panic attacks, anxiety)
- Shielded kids from business stress

## Brendan's Leadership 10 Commandments

1. **Be humble, or be humbled**
2. **Invest time in people**
3. **Achieve more, working together**
4. **Hard on the process, good on the people** (nobody wakes up wanting to do a bad job)
5. **Small improvements, every day**
6. **Seek simple solutions**
7. **Open & honest conversations, maintain confidentiality**
8. **Be curious, less judgemental**
9. **Seek clarity over certainty**
10. **No excuses** (self-reliance is King!)

## Football Captaincy Lessons

- **Youngest semi-pro captain in Queensland state league**
- **Captain of First XI at Nudgee College** — won player's player
- **Led through actions, not just words**
- **Injury mid-season** — watched from sidelines, still contributed, showed commitment
- **Key insight:** Leader doesn't need to be the best technically — their job is to get the best out of others

**Quote:** "If the sales manager is leading but is also the best performer, they aren't leading well."
