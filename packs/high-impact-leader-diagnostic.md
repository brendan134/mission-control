# High-Impact Leader Ecosystem - Diagnostic Layer

> Decision-support layer for diagnosing leadership stage, growth constraint, and next best action.

**Purpose:** Help agents identify the leader's likely stage, imbalance, broken capability area, blocked growth step, and recommended next move.

---

## Diagnostic Sequence

1. **Assess Balance** → Impact Triangle
2. **Assess Capability** → PEOPLE OS
3. **Assess Progress Step** → Growth Path
4. **Assess Identity Stage** → Leadership Stages
5. **Recommend Next Move**

---

## 1. Balance Diagnosis (Impact Triangle)

| Side | Symptoms | Likely Problem |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **Leader** | Emotional reactivity, low self-awareness, overwhelm | Leader is under-leading themselves |
| **Team** | Poor team ownership, confusion, weak accountability | Leader is under-leading the team |
| **Business** | Unclear direction, weak priorities, stalled growth | Leader is under-leading the business |

---

## 2. Capability Diagnosis (PEOPLE OS)

| Pillar | Primary Signals | Next Focus |
|--------|-----------------|------------|
| **Perceive** | Emotional reactivity, defensiveness, low self-awareness | Build self-awareness & emotional control |
| **Envision** | Unclear direction, mixed messages, decision bottlenecks | Strengthen vision & priorities |
| **Optimize** | Weak systems, inefficient workflows, repeated errors | Improve workflows & systemise |
| **Perform** | Inconsistent execution, missed deadlines, weak accountability | Build cadence & execution discipline |
| **Leverage** | Leader overinvolved, business dependency on leader | Develop people & clarify ownership |
| **Evaluate** | Repeated mistakes, poor learning loops, no reflection | Create reflection loops & adaptation |

---

## 3. Progress Diagnosis (Growth Path)

| Step | Name | Symptoms | Next Move |
|------|------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | Build Stability | Chaos, firefighting, poor cash confidence | Reduce volatility |
| 2 | Define Purpose & People | Unclear roles, weak culture, misaligned people | Clarify purpose & roles |
| 3 | Systemize for Consistency | Repeated errors, leader answering same questions | Document & standardize |
| 4 | Develop Capability | Team needs constant help, weak problem solving | Build skills & coaching |
| 5 | Create Rhythm | Inconsistency, poor follow-up, no meeting cadence | Build operating rhythms |
| 6 | Multiply Impact | Leader central to everything, growth stalling | Increase ownership & delegation |
| 7 | Build Legacy & Freedom | Business only runs with leader, no exit flexibility | Design for freedom & legacy |

---

## 4. Identity Diagnosis (Leadership Stages)

| Stage | Name | Identity | Signals |
|-------|------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Hands-On Hustler | Doer | Does too much, reactive days, little structure |
| 2 | Evolving Operator | Clarifier | Starting to create structure, still heavily involved |
| 3 | Emerging Leader | Coach | Beginning to delegate, developing people |
| 4 | Thriving Team Leader | Multiplier | Strong team ownership, team solves without help |
| 5 | High-Impact Leader | Legacy Builder | Strategic focus, leadership layers below |

---

## Decision Rules

**Priority Logic:**
1. Diagnose balance first → find most neglected side
2. Diagnose capability second → identify broken pillar
3. Diagnose progress third → find missing growth step
4. Diagnose identity fourth → estimate leadership stage
5. Recommend ONE primary next move

**Interpretation Rules:**
- If multiple pillars broken → find earliest upstream issue
- If emotional instability → prioritize PERCEIVE first
- If direction unclear → prioritize ENVISION before execution
- If systems messy → prioritize OPTIMIZE before performance
- If execution inconsistent → prioritize PERFORM
- If leader central → prioritize LEVERAGE
- If same mistakes repeat → prioritize EVALUATE

**Upstream Logic:**
- Perceive before: envision, perform, leverage
- Envision before: perform, leverage
- Optimize before: perform
- Perform before: leverage
- Evaluate after: all pillars

> - Balance Issue: **team**
> - Broken Pillar: **perform**
> - Missing Step: **Step 5 - Create Rhythm**
> - Stage: **Stage 2 - Evolving Operator**
> - **Recommendation:** Build a stronger weekly execution and accountability rhythm.

**Sample 2:**
> *Input:* "Business is growing, but owner is still involved in every major decision."
> - Balance Issue: **team**
> - Broken Pillar: **leverage**
> - Missing Step: **Step 6 - Multiply Impact**
> - Stage: **Stage 3 - Emerging Leader**
> - **Recommendation:** Shift from solving problems personally to developing stronger ownership.

## Agent Usage

**Best Use Cases:**
- Diagnose client situation
- Interpret coaching transcript
- Identify growth constraints
- Generate next step recommendation
- Personalise content/offer alignment

**Caution Rules:**
- Do not present diagnosis as absolute fact
- Use language: "likely", "appears", "suggests", "most likely"
- Recommend simplest next move first
- Don't overload with all six pillars unless requested
