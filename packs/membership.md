# Membership Pack

## High-Impact Leader Club / High-Impact Growth Community

### Program Structure
- **Membership-based** — ongoing access, not one-off
- **Two tiers or communities:**
  - High-Impact Leader Club
  - High-Impact Growth Community
- Contains both curriculum AND community components

### Community Portal
- Central hub for members
- Like "The Local Link" structure
- Peer accountability focus
- Weekly touchpoints
- Shared wins and challenges

### Curriculum Component
- Lessons inside the membership
- Self-paced learning
- Implementation focus
- Connects to community for support

### Content Flow
1. Member joins → gets portal access
2. Access curriculum at their pace
3. Engage in community for accountability
4. Implement and share wins

### Key Distinctions
- **Community** = peer interaction, accountability, engagement
- **Curriculum** = structured learning, lessons, worksheets
- **Membership** = the umbrella holding both
