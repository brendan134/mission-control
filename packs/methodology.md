# LBD IP → Methodology Mapping
## Leader By Design™ Core Intellectual Properties
### 5 Core IPs
#### 1. Leader By Design™
**Core Idea:** Leadership results come from design, not effort.
**Fixes:** People, motivation, effort
**Redesigns:** Clarity, ownership, systems, rhythm
**Core Belief:** Leadership problems are usually design problems.
**Signature Phrase:** "Leadership problems are usually design problems."
**Hook:** If everything still comes back to you, it's probably not a people problem. It's a leadership design problem.

#### 2. High-Impact Growth Path™
**Core Idea:** Businesses must grow in the correct sequence.
**7 Stages:**
1. Build Stability
2. Define Purpose & People
3. Systemise for Consistency
4. Develop Capability
5. Create Rhythm
6. Multiply Impact
7. Design Legacy & Freedom
**Core Belief:** Growth out of sequence creates stress.
**Signature Phrase:** "Growth without sequence creates stress."
**Hook:** Most businesses try to scale at Stage 6 while skipping Stages 1–4.

#### 3. Leadership Load
**Core Idea:** Leadership becomes heavy when structure is unclear.
**Problem is NOT:** Capability, motivation, effort
**Problem IS:** Decision ambiguity, ownership gaps, unclear systems
**Core Belief:** Leadership load is a design signal.
**Signature Phrase:** "Leadership load is a design signal."
**Hook:** If leadership feels heavier as your business grows, that's a signal the structure needs redesigning.

#### 4. Self-Managing Teams
**Core Idea:** Self-managing teams emerge from clarity + systems + rhythm. Not motivation.
**Core Belief:** Self-managing teams are designed, not hired.
**Signature Phrase:** "Self-managing teams are designed, not hired."
**Hook:** Self-managing teams don't emerge from trust. They emerge from structure.

#### 5. Leadership Rhythm
**Core Idea:** Consistency comes from cadence, not urgency.
**High-performing teams run on:** Weekly rhythm, monthly rhythm, quarterly rhythm
**Core Belief:** Rhythm replaces urgency.
**Signature Phrase:** "Rhythm replaces urgency."
**Hook:** Leadership rhythm replaces leadership urgency.

## PEOPLE Operating System™

### PERCEIVE
Leaders stop reacting to leadership and start leading with self-awareness.

### ENVISION
Leaders stop reacting to activity and start leading with clear direction.

### OPTIMISE
Leaders stop organising work and start engineering momentum.

### PERFORM
Leaders stop hoping for results and start driving consistent execution.

### LEVERAGE
Leaders stop carrying performance and start multiplying leadership through others.

### EVALUATE
Leaders stop reacting to results and start refining the system for greater impact.
