# Brand Voice Pack

## Core Voice
- Practical leadership, execution, accountability, team performance
- No fluff, results-focused
- Direct but warm
- "Leader" language, not "coach" language

## Tone
- Confident, not arrogant
- Action-oriented
- Experience-backed
- No jargon

## Audience
- Hands-on business owners
- Business leaders looking to step out of operations
- Building high-performing teams

## Core Outcomes We Help With
- Self-managing teams
- Stronger accountability
- Scalable performance
- Reduced owner dependence

## Words to Use
- Execution
- Accountability
- Performance
- Team
- Leverage
- Systems
- Scale

## Words to Avoid
- Journey
- Transformation
- Deep work (unless specific)
- fluffy coaching terms

## Example Phrasing
- "Build a team that runs without you"
- "Systems that scale"
- "Step out of the day-to-day"

## Positioning
Focus on practical leadership, not mindset work. We build operational capability.
