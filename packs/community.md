# Community Pack
  
## Community Portal (can be branded The Local Link or similar)
- Peer accountability group for business owners
- Weekly touchpoints
- Shared challenges and wins
  ## Engagement Style
- Direct and supportive
- Peer-to-peer, not hierarchical
- Focus on execution and accountability
- No fluff, results-oriented
  ## Content Types for Community
- Discussion prompts
- Win/share threads
- Accountability check-ins
- Resource sharing
- Event follow-up
  ## Community Posts Format
- Hook: Question or statement
- Body: Brief context or question
- CTA: Comment/answer/prompt
  ## Event Follow-Up
- Summarize key insight
- Include actionable next step
- Tag relevant members
- Clear follow-up timeline
  ## Never Do in Community
- Pitch sales aggressively
- Generic motivation posts
- Off-topic content
- Long threads without clear point
