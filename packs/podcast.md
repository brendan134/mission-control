# Podcast Pack
  
## Show Format (High-Impact Leader Podcast)
- Interview-style or solo thought leadership
- 20-30 minutes
- Real-world examples
- Actionable takeaways
  ## Episode Structure
1. Hook (problem/stakes) — 2 min
2. Guest context or topic intro — 3 min
3. Core content — 15-20 min
4. Key takeaway — 2 min
5. CTA/listen prompt — 1 min
  ## Title Formulas
- "[Topic] + [Outcome]"
- "[Number] ways to [desired result]"
- "The [adjective] truth about [topic]"
- Question format that triggers curiosity
  ## Show Notes Structure
- Episode title
- 2-3 sentence description
- Key timestamps with topics
- Links mentioned
- CTA
  ## Repurposing Rules
Each episode should be broken into:
- 1 YouTube clip (key insight)
- 3-5 LinkedIn posts (key quotes + commentary)
- 1 newsletter segment
- 1 quote graphic
- 1 short video clip (if possible)
  ## Episode Topics
Focus on:
- Team building
- Leadership execution
- Accountability
- Scaling past owner dependency
- Operational systems
