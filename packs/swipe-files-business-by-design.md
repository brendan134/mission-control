# Business By Design - Swipe Files & Resources

**Source:** James Wedmore - Business By Design

---

## Swipe File Collection

A reference library of proven marketing assets for agents to model their own funnels and campaign materials.

---

### 1. Sales Pages That Convert
- Hero/Offer section with video embed + countdown banner
- Pain-point headline: "Why your business may be doomed..."
- Audience self-identification: "Which of these apply to you?" cards
- Tactics: Countdown urgency, bold headlines, strong CTAs

### 2. Employee Mindset vs Digital CEO
**Employee Mindset:**
- Overthinking, fear of judgment
- Struggle and frustration
- Working longer hours, sleeplessness

**Digital CEO:**
- Embrace risk, trust self
- Take action, create solutions
- "Your Business Is Not Your Baby" - let go to scale

### 3. Digital CEO + Plug & Play Processes
- Business needs employee mindset shift
- Processes help businesses grow
- Learning ≠ Results - stop learning, start implementing
- "The real battle is in your head" - mindset, beliefs

### 4. Program Overview (BBD)
