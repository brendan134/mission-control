# Retrieval Packs Index

Modular knowledge packs to reduce token bloat. Load only what's needed per task.

## Available Packs

| Pack | Purpose | Load When |
|------|---------|-----------|
| `brand-voice.md` | Tone, language, positioning | Content creation, messaging |
| `messaging.md` | Hooks, reversals, belief shifts | Copy, offers, campaigns |
| `curriculum.md` | Lesson frameworks, worksheets, masterclasses | Course/lesson creation |

## Pack Relationships

**Membership content spans multiple packs:**
- `membership.md` → overall structure, portal, programs
- `curriculum.md` → lessons, courses inside membership
- `community.md` → community portal, engagement

## Usage

Load a pack by including it in context or referencing it:
- "Load Brand Voice Pack for this LinkedIn post"
- "Use Curriculum Pack to structure this lesson"

## Updating Packs

Keep packs lean:
- Remove outdated positioning
- Update after any positioning change
- Compress where possible
- Aim for <2KB per pack
