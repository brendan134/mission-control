## 1. PRICING & URGENCY CARDS

### Card 34: FREE! FREE! FREE!
**Formula:**
- Use "FREE" on opt-in pages
- Use "FREE" to describe bonuses on sales pages

**Example:** "$197 FREE Online Course for Digital Product Creators!"

---

### Card 46: YOUR PRICE = 7, THEIRS = 0
**Formula:**
- Your price ends in "7" ($997) - sounds less expensive
- Competitor cost ends in "0" ($1,000) - sounds more expensive

**Example:** "I've spent over $10,000 to learn what you are getting for just $497."

---

### Card 53: PRESUPPOSITION ACTION
**Formula:** When you [action], then [result]

**Examples:**
- "When you click the link below, you'll be granted access..."
- "When you submit your email, you'll receive..."

---

### Card 54: PRESUPPOSITION AWARENESS
**Formula:** You're about to [see/learn/witness/experience]

**Example:** "You're about to see the incredible growth our members are having inside the program."

## 2. OBJECTION HANDLING CARDS

### Card 14: REMOVE BLAME, SHAME, & GUILT
**Formula:** "[Statement]. But don't be ashamed, it's not your fault!"

**Examples:**
- "Don't feel bad, I see most entrepreneurs fall into this trap..."
- "Don't be ashamed, I totally get it. I made this same mistake myself..."

---

### Card 19: REMOVING OBJECTIONS: COUNTER EXAMPLE WITH PROOF
**Formula:** "That's what ____ thought too, until..."

**Example:** "Video might seem time consuming, and that's what Michelle thought too, until she started filming on her iPhone and got 10,000 views!"

---

### Card 20: REMOVING OBJECTIONS: ORIGINAL SOLUTION
**Formula:** "That's why I've included..."

**Example:** "Video is time-consuming, which is why I've included my video outsourcing mini-course!"

---

### Card 22: REMOVING OBJECTIONS: REVERSE IT
**Formula:** "You may think that (objection), well that's a good thing because..."

**Example:** "You may think video is time-consuming, but that's a good thing because your competitors won't do it!"

---

### Card 41: JUST AGREE!
**Formula:** Call out objection → Agree → "And..."

**Example:** "Your list is small! YES! I agree, AND it opens opportunities big lists don't have!"

---

### Card 42: THE SOLUTION FRAME
**Formula:**
- "How could you [desired outcome]?"
- "If you did have the ______, how might you ______?"

**Example:** "If you are a 'hell yes' on joining, how COULD you attract the money?"

---

## 3. STORYTELLING & FRAMING CARDS

### Card 32: THE I TO YOU SWITCHEROO
**Formula:** Switch subject from "I" to "you"

**Example:** "I used to doubt myself... then I realized **you** need to believe in yourself."

---

### Card 27: A RETURN TO YOU
**Formula:**
- "If that's how you feel..."
- "If you've been operating this way..."

**Example:** "If you've been operating your business trading time for dollars, then you know how tiring..."

---

### Card 17: THE OPEN LOOP
**Formula:**
1. Create curiosity with a "hidden problem"
2. Ask the question
3. Delay closing the loop

**Example:** "I have an idea that can help you build your business super-fast. And I'll share it in a minute. First..."

---

### Card 51: 3RD PARTY ADVICE
**Formula:** "The best advice I ever got was, [message]"

**Example:** "I'll never forget the advice my father gave me. He said, 'Always pay your people.'"

---

### Card 52: THE RIPPLE EFFECT
**Formula:** Show how solving one problem improves other areas of life

**Example:** "Healing money blocks isn't just about making more money—it's about a higher quality of life with those you care about."

---

### Card 2: INTRODUCING YOUR STORY THE RIGHT WAY
**Formula:** "If you've ever experienced __________, then chances are you can relate..."

---

### Card 28: MAKING TESTIMONIALS RELEVANT
**Formula:**
- "What if this were your story too?"
- "Can you imagine what it would be like if ____?"

## 4. PSYCHOLOGICAL TRIGGERS CARDS

### Card 50: SAY THE QUIET PART OUT LOUD
**Formula:**
- "You may even be thinking, [add thought]"
- "Or maybe that little voice in your head is saying..."

**Example:** "What?! That's crazy pants!" — speak to reader's internal thoughts

---

### Card 39: THE POWER OF THE QUESTION
**Formula:** Turn bold statements into questions:
- "Have you ever considered..."
- "Do you ever wonder why..."
- "Why is it that..."

**Example:** "Have you ever considered why hiring a copywriter is such a big mistake?"

---

### Card 48: MAKE A PROMISE AS A QUESTION
**Formula:**
- "How would you like to [Promise]?"
- "How valuable would it be to [Promise]?"
- "What would it be like to [Promise]?"

---

### Card 16: QUESTIONS THAT CAN'T BE ANSWERED WITHOUT READING ON
**Formula:** Instead of "Do any of these sound familiar?" use "Which of these apply to you?"

---

### Card 24: SO IF YOU AGREE...
**Formula:** "[Statement of belief], so if you agree, let's continue"

**Example:** "So if you agree that vision is most important, then let's move on."

---

### Card 23: POSITIVE IDENTITY
**Formula:** [Desired Identity] linked to [behavior/belief]

**Example:** "Being a smart entrepreneur, I know this is not a new concept for you..."

---

### Card 21: THE FACT... MEANS...
**Formula:** "The fact that you..."

**Example:** "The fact that you've made it this far means you have a commitment to growing your business..."

---

### Card 18: HOW TO DISMANTLE A BELIEF!
**Formula:**
1. Identify the BELIEF
2. Break into A and B sections (A = B)
3. Find a Counter Example (A but not B, or B but not A)

**Example:** "How many people do you know that work hard and have nothing to show for it?"

---

### Card 33: NEVER READ MINDS
**Formula:** Replace assumptions with "IF"
- "If... then..."
- "Perhaps... then..."
- "Does this sound like you?"

---

### Card 4: THE CHOICE-BIND
**Formula:** "If you are really serious about [Desired Result], you have to make one of two choices right now..."

**Example:** "You can either keep doing what you've been doing... OR you can watch this series and finally discover how to..."

---

### Card 3: GET VS GIVING
**Formula:** "If you are [Type of Person] who wants [Outcome], you're about to GET..."

---

### Card 1: ELICITING THE BENEFIT
**Formula:** [Tangible Result] SO THAT [Feeling State or Bigger Outcome]

**Example:** "...get your message in front of more people so you can live the life you want..."

## 5. FORMATTING & STYLE CARDS

### Card 38: KEEP IT 4TH GRADE LEVEL
**Rules:**
- No 4+ syllable words
- No run-on sentences
- No complicated concepts

---

### Card 29: KEEPIN' IT CONVERSATIONAL
**Formula:**
- "Ya know what I mean?"
- "Right?"
- "Who am I kidding?!"

**Example:** "What?! That's crazy pants! I know!"

---

### Card 30: THE P.P.S. (POWERFUL POST SCRIPT)
**Formula:** "P.S. [Your powerful post script]"

Use at the end of emails, sales pages, Instagram posts to reiterate CTA.

---

### Card 35: BEING VISUAL!
**Visual Cues:** Look! Can you see/picture/imagine? Is it clear? Bright future, focus, notice, watch

---

### Card 36: LET THEM HEAR YOU!
**Auditory Words:** sounds like, I can hear, resonating/clicking, listen for, ring a bell?

---

### Card 37: LET THEM FEEL YOU!
**Kinesthetic Words:** feels like, connect, heavy, grasp, experience, touch

---

### Card 43: ASSUME THEY ARE SMART
**Formula:**
- "You and I know both know..."
- "I'm sure you can agree..."
- "I don't have to tell you..."

---

### Card 47: SHOW DON'T TELL
**Formula:**
1. Identify the emotion you want reader to feel
2. Write scenario that evokes it
3. Include deepest thoughts

**Example:** Instead of "You're overwhelmed" → "Here's what you're up against: learn copy, social media, email marketing, tech just to see your first sale!"

## 6. ADVANCED TECHNIQUES CARDS

### Card 44: THE EMBEDDED COMMAND
**Formulas:**
- "When you [command], you will [benefit]"
- "You don't have to [command] until..."
- "Don't even think about _____ until you _____"
- "You don't have to ____ to see ____"

---

### Card 45: THE EMBEDDED COMMAND BIND
**Formula:** "Would you rather/like to [command 1] or [command 2]?"

**Example:** "Would you like to invest with the payment plan or save and pay in full?"

---

### Card 49: THE HEADLINE CARD
**6 Templates:**
1. "Discover How to [Result] without [Objection]!"
2. "How One [Identity] Generated [Result] in [Time]!"
3. "Give Me [Time] and I'll Show You How to [Result]!"
4. "Get [Result] in [Time] or [Guarantee]"
5. "[Number] Ways/Things That [Result]!"
6. "How to Overcome [Problem] with [Vehicle]"

---

### Card 26: CREATING A POSITIVE FUTURE FRAME
**Formula:**
- "What would that do..."
- "Imagine if (positive frame)..."

**Example:** "Imagine how much faster you'll grow when you're surrounded by hundreds of people doing the same thing..."

---

### Card 25: METAPHOR
**Formula:** "[Thing] is like [Other Thing]"

**Example:** "You wouldn't captain a ship without a rudder... so why do it in your business?"

---

### Card 31: IMAGINE...
**Formula:**
- "Imagine...[Desired Outcome]"
- "Can you imagine...[Desired Outcome]?"
- "Imagine...[Undesired Outcome + Unwanted Behavior]"

**Note:** "Just suppose" is a great synonym for "imagine"

---

### Card 40: EMULATING EXPERTS
**Formula:** [Expert/Category] linked to behavior or belief

**Example:** "Every single successful online expert has an irresistible offer. Can you think of one who became successful with a BAD offer?"

---

# Usage Tips for Agents

1. **Match the card to the situation:** Use objection handling cards when addressing concerns, storytelling cards when building connection.

2. **Layer techniques:** Combine multiple cards for maximum impact (e.g., Open Loop + Imagine + Embedded Command).

3. **Test and iterate:** Not every card works for every audience. Track what resonates.

4. **Stay authentic:** These are frameworks, not formulas to copy blindly. Make them yours.
